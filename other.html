Other Charaters

The Loud House And The Casagrandes

Sid

Sid is a very bubbly and sweet girl, and a very nice friend to Ronnie Anne. Just like Ronnie Anne, she and her family had just moved to the city as of "Friended! with the Casagrandes". She is shown to be into trying out everything she sees her peers doing in the city. She enjoys various activities such as eating hot dogs for breakfast and skateboarding after seeing Ronnie Anne doing them.

Nikki

Nikki is part of a group of friends with Sameer and Casey. She is also friends with Ronnie Anne and Lincoln, even after she confesses to the trio about being from Royal Woods, along with Lincoln. She also rides a purple skateboard.

Sameer

Sameer is part of a group of friends with Nikki and Casey. He is also friends with Ronnie Anne and Lincoln, even after the former confesses to the trio about really being from Royal Woods, along with Lincoln. He also rides a green skateboard with yellow wheels.

Casey

Casey is part of a group of friends with Sameer and Nikki. He is also friends with Ronnie Anne and Lincoln, even after she confesses to the trio about really being from Royal Woods, along with Lincoln. He rides a green bicycle.

Clyde

Clyde is awkward, caring, nerdy, sweet, and supportive at the same time. Lincoln can always count on Clyde to help him with a plan. Like Lincoln, Clyde has a passion for comic books, video games, and science fiction movies. He is also a cat lover and owns two cats that he loves very much, even though his oldest cat Cleopawtra always misbehaves.

Stella

Stella Zhau comes from a family of Filipino descent. Her family moved to Royal Woods and Stella was registered to Royal Woods Elementary School.

Liam

So far, not much known is about him, other than that he is one of Lincoln's best friends. He likes basketball and rock music. He seems to be from the countryside given his accent and during "Back Out There" where he mentions doing stereotypical agricultural activities such as milking cows and that donkey poo was hard to get out.

Zach

Zach is the son of two parents whom are both obsessed with aliens. According to Lincoln, Zach lives between a circus and a freeway.

Rusty

Rusty Spokes is one of Lincoln's classmates and a friend to him. He is also a member of the local bicycle gang. He speaks with a squeaky, high-pitched voice.

Rocy

Rocky is the younger brother of Rusty, who is Lincoln's classmate. Rocky is also one of Lucy's love interests,Rocky appears to be a rather quiet kid, with an interest in video games and sports. Despite his indifferent demeanor, he seems to be polite and nice, given his friendly interactions with Lincoln.
